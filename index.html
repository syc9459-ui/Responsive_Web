Phoebe Bridgers

Phoebe Bridgers is an American singer-songwriter known for her emotional indie folk sound and deeply personal lyrics. She was born in California and started releasing music in her early twenties, quickly gaining attention for her debut album Stranger in the Alps. Her second album, Punisher, made her even more well-known and earned multiple Grammy nominations.

Beyond her solo work, she has collaborated with other artists and been part of groups like boygenius and Better Oblivion Community Center. She is also known for her simple, moody aesthetic, often wearing a skeleton costume on stage.
